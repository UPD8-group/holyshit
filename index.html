The Story

Born at a Ball.
Built for Everyone.

HolyShit.app started as a conversation at a black tie ball. A friend who runs mentioned she'd never found a good way to plan routes around bathroom access. By the time the band finished their second set, the concept was locked in.

Free for users. Useful for athletes. Built for real life. We're Australian, we don't take ourselves too seriously, and we think finding a toilet when you desperately need one should be the easiest thing in the world.

Hence the name. Hence the 💩.
