What HolyShit.app is

HolyShit.app is a free, community-assisted toilet and facility finder for Australia. It is operated by UPD8 Group Pty Ltd, a Canberra-based software company. The app helps people locate nearby toilets, service stations, fast food venues and other facilities with accessible bathrooms.

The app is free to use and requires no account or registration.

Why it exists

We built HolyShit.app because finding a toilet when you actually need one — mid-run, on a road trip, with kids in tow, in an unfamiliar suburb — is genuinely hard. Existing tools are buried in government websites or require searching through Google Maps one by one.

We wanted something fast, honest and built for the moment. So we built it ourselves.

Accuracy of data

Our facility data comes from multiple sources including the Australian Government's public toilet dataset, OpenStreetMap contributors, and community reports submitted through the app.

We make every effort to keep data accurate, but we cannot guarantee that every listed facility will be open, clean, or accessible when you arrive. Always have a backup plan. 💩

Facilities change — they close, get renovated, change hours. If you find incorrect information, please use the Report button in the app to let us know. Your reports directly improve the experience for everyone.
